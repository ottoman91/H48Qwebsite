In the interests of making this electronic version of the Final Report available as quickly as possible it was produced in a tearing hurry and the process of converting between computer systems and different file formats may have introduced some errors of a minor nature. Specifically, fonts and typefaces may vary, footnote numbers may appear without their text, cross references may not be accurate, some diagrams and charts have still to be converted and tables, such as the Balance Sheets, tend not to line up.
